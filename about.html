BIO

About me

I am a 20-something-year-old man who grew up between France and the Netherlands. Creativity has always interested me, whether it was building with Lego as a kid, followed by spending evenings working on Minecraft projects as a teen, starting with photography and finally teaching myself how to use Photoshop while at university as a hobby.

This creativity is also tied to a desire to explore both the real world as well as my own imagination in an attempt to see and document those things that people rarely think about.
