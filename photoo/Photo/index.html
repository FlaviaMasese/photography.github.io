About my Art

My drive to explore is also one of my main drives behind creating photos and artwork. The most appealing aspect of creating art for me is the ability to portray beauty in places or ideas which are not necessarily seen as beautiful in order to create something unique.

Even something completely unremarkable and mundane can become beautiful and unique if you’re using the right tools. I also like to incorporate images into my work which might not be conventionally beautiful or even a bit ugly by themselves but to incorporate them in such a way that it becomes part of a nicer whole, simply because you can’t have beauty without ugliness. Finding a balance between these two is essential.
